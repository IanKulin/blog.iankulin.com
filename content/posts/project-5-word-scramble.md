Another [100 Days of Swift UI](https://www.hackingwithswift.com/100/swiftui) project wrapped up - this time a scrabble like word game. New techniques included saving a large text file in the app bundle and loading it (via a string) into an array on launch of the view. Also a short adventure into UIKit to use a UITextChecker.

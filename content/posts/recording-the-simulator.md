I've been using Quicktime to screen record the preview or simulator to document my work here and in Github ReadMe's, but thanks to this [excellent short post](https://sarunw.com/posts/record-ios-simulator-video-and-gif-with-xcode/) by [Sarun W](https://twitter.com/sarunw), I now know it's possible to do that directly from the simulator, including capturing gestures, and exporting to animated gifs!

If you're interested in how generative AI works, check out [Ishan Anand](https://ishananand.com/)'s Youtube series "[Spreadsheets are all you need](https://www.youtube.com/@Spreadsheetsareallyouneed)". He steps through the basics using an Excel spreadsheet that encompasses most of GPT-2. Just doing that is an impressive (and hilarious) feat, but he also has a knack for teaching, so you'll come away with a good understanding of AI and how some of it's limitations manifest.

Ishan is selling a course, which I guess these are the first three lessons of, and I got a lot out of them. It's also possible to [download the spreadsheet](https://github.com/ianand/spreadsheets-are-all-you-need/releases/tag/v0.6.1) he uses in the course to play with.

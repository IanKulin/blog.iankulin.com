Another advantage of the videos, that hadn't occurred to me when I [mentioned it the other day](/cs193p/), is learning the correct pronunciation of things you've only ever read in books.

Apparently, tuple is pronounced two-pull, and not with the tup to rhyme with cup as I'd always imagined. Google has confirmed, so it's not just a UK thing.

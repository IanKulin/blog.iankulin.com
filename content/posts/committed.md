I quite like logging into GitHub and seeing my commit history as the graph with the green dots. Once I get up to a year it would be a great thing to have on a T-Shirt.

![](/images/screen-shot-2022-12-03-at-7.36.29-pm.jpg)

I'd expect to be seeing the busy weekends, but Tuesday nights seem to be oddly productive. It could just be a start of the week energy thing - I have some other community obligations on a couple of Monday nights a month.

git is an amazing tool - better that the commercial tool I used when I was programming a long way back. I really admire what the many open source programmers have put in to it. I also owe some gratitude to Microsoft for making GitHub available to newbs like me at no cost - it's an great service.

According to the graph, my first commits were on July 10. My first blog post was on the 5th, and I'm on a 154 once per day posting streak - although there's a few two per day scattered through there when I've been on holidays. I'm only up to day 64 of the 100 Days of SwiftUI - so I clearly I haven't been getting in an hour each day on that religiously, but overall, I'm pretty happy with my commitment to myself.
